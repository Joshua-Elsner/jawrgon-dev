How to Play

Welcome to JAWRGON!
Your goal is to guess the Shark's secret 5-letter word in six tries or less. After each guess, the bubbles change color: Green is correct, yellow means wrong spot, and gray means not in the word.

Yoink!
Everyone guesses at the same time! If you guess correctly, you survive, become the Shark, and set the next word. But don't be a pokey fish! If someone else guesses it before you, they yoink the win, and your board resets!

Leaderboard & Shark of the Week:
Your rank is based on how long you maintain your reign as the Shark. The leaderboard resets every Sunday at midnight, and the player with the most Time as Shark is crowned the Shark of the Week!
