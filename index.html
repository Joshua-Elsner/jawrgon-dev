What is Daredevil Challenge?

Wager up to 5 of your points for a bonus payout if you guess the word. HOWEVER! For every point you wager, you sacrifice one of your six guesses. For example, betting five points means you get only one single guess at the shark's secret word!

How to Play

Welcome to JAWRGON! Your goal is to guess the shark's secret 5-letter word in six tries or less. After each guess, the bubbles will change color: Green means the letter is correctly placed, yellow means it's in the word but the wrong spot, and gray means it isn't in the word at all. If you guess correctly, you become the shark and get to set the next word for everyone to guess. You will earn points for every incorrect guess your opponents make, and earn a Fish Eaten leaderboard bonus if you completely stump them.
