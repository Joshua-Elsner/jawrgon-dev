How to Play

Welcome to JAWRGON! Your goal is to guess the shark's secret 5-letter word in six tries or less. After each guess, the bubbles will change color: Green means the letter is correctly placed, yellow means it's in the word but the wrong spot, and gray means it isn't in the word at all. If you guess correctly, you survive, become the shark, and get to set the next word. Your rank on the leaderboard is determined by how long you can maintain your reign as the Shark.
